## Color/Tone Suggestions

**Avoid:**
- Bright, saturated colors (feels gimmicky)
- Too much purple/pink (every AI tool uses this)
- Corporate blue (feels enterprise, not indie)

**Consider:**
- Dark mode default (developers prefer)
- Muted, professional palette
- Green for "build signals" (positive)
- Red for "pain points" (attention)
- Yellow for "anxiety barriers" (caution)
- One accent color for CTAs (orange, teal, or deep blue)

**Typography:**
- Sans-serif (Inter, Geist, or similar)
- Good readability (18px minimum body text)
- Clear hierarchy (headlines 2-3x larger than body)
